Our Story

Built in Pudukkottai, run on precision.

Sharp Tool Tech began as a focused proprietorship in Pudukkottai, Tamil Nadu, with a simple premise — machine shops shouldn't have to compromise between catalogue tooling and the exact tool their component actually needs.

Fourteen-plus years on, that premise still runs the workshop. Under proprietor B. Subramanian, our small, expert-led team specializes in indexable toolings, tailor-made combination boring tools, and tool holders engineered to accept any major insert brand — so switching to us never means switching your inventory.

We remain intentionally lean: a compact team of skilled hands, the latest machinery, and a direct line between the person cutting your tool and the person who designed it.

What Drives Us

Mission, vision & the standard we hold.

Mission

To manufacture indexable tooling and tailor-made combination boring tools that give every customer the exact fit their component demands — engineered, not adapted.

Vision

To be the tool room manufacturers in Tamil Nadu turn to first for tooling that has to work exactly right, the first time.

Quality Commitment

Every holder is machined, checked and matched to its intended insert brand before it leaves our facility — no exceptions.

Core Values

Direct relationships, honest lead times, and tooling engineered around your machine — not a generic spec sheet.

Infrastructure

A workshop built to hold tight tolerances.

Our manufacturing unit is equipped with the latest machinery, capable of producing the full range of turners, groovers, CNC tools and millers our customers ask for.

Before a single tool is machined, our team analyzes the customer's machine capacity, available speeds & feeds, and required productivity — then justifies the cost reduction achieved by replacing the tools currently in use. It's a slower first step, and it's why the tooling fits the first time.
