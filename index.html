Who We Are

A tool room's discipline, applied to every insert we hold.

Sharp Tool Tech is a Pudukkottai-based manufacturer specializing in indexable tooling — P-Type, S-Type and M-Type tool holders in square and round shank, miniature boring bars, and back recess tools for chamfering and boring.

We also engineer CNC sleeves, and threading, parting & face grooving tool holders — with every tailor-made combination boring tool built to suit milling and turning centers, and special purpose machines (SPM).

Common Questions

Frequently asked

Can you machine tool holders for our existing insert brand?

Yes — our tool holders are engineered to accept Sandvik, Korloy, Mitsubishi, Tungaloy, Widia, Iscar or any standard ISO insert, so you don't need to change your existing inventory.

Do you build fully custom, tailor-made tooling?

Yes. Tailor-made combination boring tools are one of our core specialities — built for milling and turning centers as well as special purpose machines (SPM), based on your component drawing.

What shank options are available on your tool holders?

Our indexable tool holders are available in both square shank and round shank configurations, across P-Type, S-Type and M-Type profiles.

How do I get a quote for a specific tool?

Use the Request Quote form on our Contact page, call us directly, or reach out on WhatsApp — share your drawing or component specification and our team will respond with pricing and lead time.

Where is Sharp Tool Tech located?

Our manufacturing facility is based in Pudukkottai, Tamil Nadu, India. We ship tooling across India on request.
